------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR the UK public authorities as well as the courts
TO treat the Articles in Schedule 1 of the Act, as having the force of law in the UK
SUBJECT TO Section 3 of the Act
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR Courts, Administrative bodies, and interpreters of legislation
TO construe the specific terms in the Vienna Convention,('agents of the receiving State' includes constables; 'national' means UK and colonies; etc.) in accordance with definitions that are provided in this subsection
WHEN/IF/WHERE interpreting and applying the Vienna Convention in UK legal proceedings
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR Courts and Administrative Authorities
TO treat any waiver by the head of a mission (or acting head) as a waiver by the state.
WHEN/IF/WHERE Applying Article 32 of the Convention
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR Her Majesty by Order in Council
TO withdraw privileges and immunities from the mission or another state or its members
ONLY IF the immunities or the privileges accorded to a UK mission or its members in the State are less than those conferred by the Act.
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR Courts and parties in proceedings under the Act
TO treat a certificate issued by or under the authority of Secretary of State as conclusive evidence of the stated fact.
WHEN/IF/WHERE [WHEN] a question arises in proceedings about entitlement to any privilege or immunity under this act
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR the officials or the government making recommendations to Her Majesty
TO ensure a draft Order under section 2 is laid before and approved by resolution by each House of Parliament
BEFORE making the recommendation to Her Majesty in Council
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR either House of Parliament
TO annul a statutory instrument containing an Order under Section 3
WHEN/IF/WHERE [WHEN] a resolution for annulment is passed.
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR Her Majesty by Order in Council
TO vary or revoke any Order made under the Act
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR authorities in the UK
TO extend diplomatic  immunities and exemptions to persons or goods as provided in any such agreement
WHEN/IF/WHERE [WHEN] such an agreement was in force at the commencement of this Act and remains in force
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR the Secretary of State
TO publish and amend notices in the London, Edinburgh and Belfast Gazettes specifying States and classes of persons subject to applicable agreements
WHEN/IF/WHERE [WHEN] such agreements are either in force or amended
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR Her Majesty by Order in Council
TO appoint the day this Act comes into force
