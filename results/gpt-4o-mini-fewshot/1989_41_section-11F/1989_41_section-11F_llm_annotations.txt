IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR an individual
TO take part in an activity that is expected to help to establish, maintain or improve the involvement of that or another individual in the life of a child who is ordinarily resident in Wales
WHEN/IF/WHERE required by an activity direction or condition
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR an individual
TO receive assistance
UNLESS the individual satisfies such conditions as regards his financial resources as may be set out in the regulations
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR an individual
TO receive assistance
UNLESS the activity in which the individual is required to take part is provided to him in England or Wales
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR an individual
TO receive assistance
UNLESS where the activity in which the individual is required to take part is provided to him in England, it is provided by a person who is for the time being approved by the Secretary of State as a provider of activities required by an activity direction or condition
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR an individual
TO receive assistance
UNLESS where the activity in which the individual is required to take part is provided to him in Wales, it is provided by a person who is for the time being approved by the National Assembly for Wales as a provider of activities required by an activity direction or condition
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR regulations under this section
TO provide that no assistance is available to an individual
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR regulations under this section
TO make provision as to the maximum amount of assistance that may be paid to or in respect of an individual
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR regulations under this section
TO make provision where the amount may vary according to an individual's financial resources, as to the method by which the amount is to be determined
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR regulations under this section
TO authorise payments by way of assistance to be made directly to persons providing activities required by an activity direction or condition
