IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR an individual who is a child
TO have an activity condition imposed on them
ONLY IF the individual is a parent of the child concerned [source: https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/1989/41/section/11D]
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR a child arrangements order
TO impose an activity condition if it is an excepted order
WHEN/IF/WHERE the child arrangements order is an excepted order (within the meaning given by section 11B(4)) [source: https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/1989/41/section/11D]
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR an individual
TO have an activity condition imposed on them
ONLY IF the individual is not habitually resident in England and Wales [source: https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/1989/41/section/11D]
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR an activity condition
TO cease to have effect
WHEN/IF/WHERE the individual subject to the condition ceases to be habitually resident in England and Wales [source: https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/1989/41/section/11D]
