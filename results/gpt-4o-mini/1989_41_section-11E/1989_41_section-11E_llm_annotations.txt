IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR court
TO satisfy itself as to the matters falling within subsections (2) to (4)
BEFORE making an activity direction (or imposing an activity condition by means of a child arrangements order)
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR court
TO obtain and consider information about the individual who would be subject to the direction (or the condition) and the likely effect of the direction (or the condition) on him
BEFORE making such a direction (or such an order)
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR officer of the Service or Welsh family proceedings officer
TO comply with any such request for information as to the matters in subsections (2) to (5)
ONLY IF asked by the court
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR court
TO consider information about any conflict with the individual's religious beliefs
IF the likely effect of the direction (or the condition)
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR court
TO consider information about any interference with the times at which the individual normally works or attends an educational establishment
IF the likely effect of the direction (or the condition)
