FOR the court

FOR individual falling within subsection (3)
TO take such steps as may be specified in the order
SUBJECT TO with a view to enabling the officer of the Service or Welsh family proceedings officer to comply with the court's request under subsection (2)
UNLESS the individual is a child unless he is a parent of the child with respect to whom the order falling within subsection (1) was made
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR the court
TO make a request under subsection (2) in relation to a child arrangements order that is an excepted order
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR the court
TO make a request under subsection (2)
WHEN on making the child arrangements order (or the order varying the child arrangements order)
OR at any time during the subsequent course of the proceedings as they relate to contact with the child concerned or to the child's living arrangements
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR the court

IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR the court
TO report to the court on such matters relating to the individual's compliance as the court may specify in the request
