IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR individual who is a child
TO have an activity condition imposed on them
ONLY IF the individual is a parent of the child concerned
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR child arrangements order
TO impose an activity condition
IF the child arrangements order is an excepted order (within the meaning given by section 11B(4)) [source: 11B(4)]
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR individual
TO have an activity condition imposed on them
ONLY IF the individual is habitually resident in England and Wales
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR activity condition
TO cease to have effect
IF the individual subject to the condition ceases to be habitually resident in England and Wales
