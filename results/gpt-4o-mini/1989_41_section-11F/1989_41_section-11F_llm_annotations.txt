IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR individuals falling within subsection (2)

IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR National Assembly for Wales
TO make payments to assist individuals falling within subsection (4) in paying relevant charges or fees
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR individuals falling within subsection (4)
TO take part in an activity that is expected to help to establish, maintain or improve the involvement of that or another individual in the life of a child who is ordinarily resident in Wales

IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR individuals
TO satisfy such conditions as regards his financial resources as may be set out in the regulations
UNLESS no assistance is available to an individual unless
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR individuals
TO take part in an activity provided to him in England or Wales
UNLESS no assistance is available to an individual unless the activity in which the individual is required by an activity direction or condition to take part is provided to him in England or Wales
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR individuals
TO take part in an activity provided by a person approved by the Secretary of State or National Assembly for Wales
UNLESS no assistance is available to an individual unless where the activity in which the individual is required to take part is provided to him in England, it is provided by a person who is for the time being approved by the Secretary of State as a provider of activities required by an activity direction or condition
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR individuals
TO take part in an activity provided by a person approved by the National Assembly for Wales
UNLESS no assistance is available to an individual unless where the activity in which the individual is required to take part is provided to him in Wales, it is provided by a person who is for the time being approved by the National Assembly for Wales as a provider of activities required by an activity direction or condition
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR regulations under this section
TO make provision as to the maximum amount of assistance that may be paid to or in respect of an individual
ONLY IF the individual is required by an activity direction or condition to take part
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR regulations under this section
TO authorise payments by way of assistance to be made directly to persons providing activities required by an activity direction or condition
