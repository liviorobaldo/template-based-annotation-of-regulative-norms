IT IS PERMITTED
FOR the court
TO make a section 8 order with respect to the child
WHEN a question arises with respect to the welfare of any child [source: section-10-1]
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR the court
TO make a section 8 order even though no application has been made
WHEN the court considers that the order should be made [source: section-10-1-b]
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR the court
TO make a section 8 order on the application of a person
WHEN the person is entitled to apply for a section 8 order or has obtained the leave of the court [source: section-10-2]
------------------------
IT IS SUBJECT TO
FOR this section
TO the restrictions imposed by section 9
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR any parent, guardian, or special guardian of the child
TO apply to the court for any section 8 order with respect to a child
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR any party to a marriage or civil partnership in relation to whom the child is a child of the family
TO apply for a child arrangements order with respect to a child
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR a local authority foster parent
TO apply for a child arrangements order
IF the child has lived with him for a period of at least one year immediately preceding the application [source: section-10-5A]
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR a relative of a child
TO apply for a child arrangements order
IF the child has lived with the relative for a period of at least one year immediately preceding the application [source: section-10-5B]
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR a person who would not otherwise be entitled
TO apply for the variation or discharge of a section 8 order
IF the order was made on his application or he is named in provisions of the order [source: section-10-6]
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR any person who falls within a category of person prescribed by rules of court
TO apply for any such section 8 order as may be prescribed
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR an application for a child arrangements order
TO be made with respect to a child
ONLY IF the leave of the court is obtained [source: section-10-7A]
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR the court
TO grant leave to a child to make an application for a section 8 order
ONLY IF the court is satisfied that the child has sufficient understanding [source: section-10-8]
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR the court
TO have particular regard to specified factors when deciding whether to grant leave
WHEN the person applying for leave is not the child concerned [source: section-10-9]
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR a person
TO apply for a child arrangements order
WHEN the period of three years mentioned need not be continuous but must not have begun more than five years before, or ended more than three months before, the making of the application [source: section-10-10]
