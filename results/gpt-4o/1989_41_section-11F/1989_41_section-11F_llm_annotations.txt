IT IS PERMITTED
FOR Secretary of State
TO make payments to assist individuals in paying relevant charges or fees
WHEN individuals fall within subsection (2) [source: 11F(2)]
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR National Assembly for Wales
TO make payments to assist individuals in paying relevant charges or fees
WHEN individuals fall within subsection (4) [source: 11F(4)]
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR individual
TO receive assistance
UNLESS the individual satisfies such conditions as regards his financial resources as may be set out in the regulations
UNLESS the activity is provided to him in England or Wales
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR individual
TO receive assistance for activities in England
UNLESS the activity is provided by a person approved by the Secretary of State
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR individual
TO receive assistance for activities in Wales
UNLESS the activity is provided by a person approved by the National Assembly for Wales
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR regulations
TO make provision as to the maximum amount of assistance that may be paid
WHEN an individual is required by an activity direction or condition to take part in an activity
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR regulations
TO vary the amount of assistance according to an individual's financial resources
AS TO the method by which the amount is to be determined
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR regulations
TO authorize payments by way of assistance to be made directly to persons providing activities
WHEN activities are required by an activity direction or condition
