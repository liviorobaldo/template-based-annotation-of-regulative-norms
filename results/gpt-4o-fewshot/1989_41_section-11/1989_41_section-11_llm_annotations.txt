IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR the court
TO draw up a timetable with a view to determining the question without delay
WHEN/IF/WHERE in proceedings in which any question of making a section 8 order, or any other question with respect to such an order, arises [source: section-11-1]
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR the court
TO give such directions as it considers appropriate for the purpose of ensuring, so far as is reasonably practicable, that that timetable is adhered to
WHEN/IF/WHERE in proceedings in which any question of making a section 8 order, or any other question with respect to such an order, arises [source: section-11-1]
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR rules of court
TO specify periods within which specified steps must be taken in relation to proceedings in which such questions arise
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR rules of court
TO make other provision with respect to such proceedings for the purpose of ensuring, so far as is reasonably practicable, that such questions are determined without delay
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR a court
TO make a section 8 order at any time during the course of the proceedings in question
EVEN THOUGH it is not in a position to dispose finally of those proceedings [source: section-11-3]
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR a child arrangements order
TO have effect
WHEN/IF/WHERE the order has the result that there are times when the child lives or is to live with one of the parents [source: section-11-5]
IF the parents live together for a continuous period of more than six months [source: section-11-5]
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR a child arrangements order
TO have effect
WHEN/IF/WHERE it provides for the child to spend time or otherwise have contact with one of the child's parents at times when the child is living with the child's other parent [source: section-11-6]
IF the parents live together for a continuous period of more than six months [source: section-11-6]
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR a section 8 order
TO contain directions about how it is to be carried into effect
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR a section 8 order
TO impose conditions which must be complied with by any person named in the order
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR a section 8 order
TO be made to have effect for a specified period, or contain provisions which are to have effect for a specified period
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR a section 8 order
TO make such incidental, supplemental or consequential provision as the court thinks fit
