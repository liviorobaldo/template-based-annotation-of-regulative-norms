FOR The National Assembly for Wales
TO make payments to assist individuals in paying relevant charges or fees

IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR an individual
TO receive assistance
UNLESS the individual satisfies such conditions as regards his financial resources as may be set out in the regulations [source: https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/1989/41/section/11F]
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR an individual
TO receive assistance
UNLESS the activity in which the individual is required to take part is provided to him in England or Wales [source: https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/1989/41/section/11F]
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR an individual
TO receive assistance for an activity provided in England
UNLESS it is provided by a person who is for the time being approved by the Secretary of State as a provider of activities required by an activity direction or condition [source: https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/1989/41/section/11F]
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR an individual
TO receive assistance for an activity provided in Wales
UNLESS it is provided by a person who is for the time being approved by the National Assembly for Wales as a provider of activities required by an activity direction or condition [source: https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/1989/41/section/11F]
