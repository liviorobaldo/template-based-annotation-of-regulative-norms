------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR court
TO consider the child’s welfare as the paramount consideration
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR court
TO have regard to the general principle that any delay in determining the question is likely to prejudice the welfare of the child
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR court
TO not make the order or any of the orders unless it considers that doing so would be better for the child than making no order at all
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR father and mother
TO each have parental responsibility for the child
WHEN a child’s father and mother were married to, or civil partners of, each other at the time of his birth
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR mother
TO have parental responsibility for the child
WHEN a child’s father and mother were not married to, or civil partners of, each other at the time of his birth
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR father
TO have parental responsibility for the child if he has acquired it in accordance with the provisions of this Act
WHEN the father shall have parental responsibility for the child if he has acquired it
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR person with parental responsibility
TO cease to have that responsibility solely because some other person subsequently acquires parental responsibility for the child
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR each person with parental responsibility
TO act alone and without the other (or others) in meeting that responsibility
UNLESS nothing in this Part shall be taken to affect the operation of any enactment which requires the consent of more than one person in a matter affecting the child
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR person with parental responsibility
TO act in any way which would be incompatible with any order made with respect to the child under this Act
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR person with parental responsibility
TO surrender or transfer any part of that responsibility to another
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR father
TO acquire parental responsibility for the child if he meets certain conditions
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR parental responsibility agreement
TO have effect unless it is made in the form prescribed by regulations made by the Lord Chancellor
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR parental responsibility agreement
TO have effect unless it is recorded in the prescribed manner
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR person who has acquired parental responsibility
TO cease to have that responsibility only if the court so orders
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR court
TO appoint an individual to be the child’s guardian
WHEN the child has no parent with parental responsibility for him
OR a parent, guardian or special guardian of the child's was named in a child arrangements order as a person with whom the child was to live and has died while the order was in force
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR parent with parental responsibility
TO appoint another individual to be the child’s guardian in the event of his death
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR guardian appointed under section 5
TO have parental responsibility for the child concerned
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR authority or officer of the Service or a Welsh family proceedings officer
TO comply with any request for a report under this section
