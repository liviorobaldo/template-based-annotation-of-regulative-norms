------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR court
TO consider the child's welfare as the paramount consideration
WHEN determining any question with respect to the upbringing of a child or the administration of a child’s property [source: section-1-1]
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR court
TO have regard to the general principle that any delay in determining the question is likely to prejudice the welfare of the child
IN any proceedings in which any question with respect to the upbringing of a child arises [source: section-1-2]
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR court
TO presume that involvement of each parent will further the child's welfare
UNLESS the contrary is shown [source: section-1-2A]
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR court
TO have regard in particular to the child's wishes, needs, and other factors
IN the circumstances mentioned in subsection (4) [source: section-1-3]
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR court
TO make an order unless it considers that doing so would be better for the child than making no order at all
WHERE a court is considering whether or not to make one or more orders under this Act with respect to a child [source: section-1-5]
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR father and mother
TO have parental responsibility for the child
WHERE they were married to, or civil partners of, each other at the time of the child's birth [source: section-2-1]
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR mother
TO have parental responsibility for the child
WHERE the child's father and mother were not married to, or civil partners of, each other at the time of his birth [source: section-2-2]
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR father
TO have parental responsibility for the child
IF he has acquired it in accordance with the provisions of this Act [source: section-2-2]
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR person with parental responsibility
TO act alone in meeting that responsibility
WHERE more than one person has parental responsibility for a child [source: section-2-7]
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR person with parental responsibility
TO act in any way which would be incompatible with any order made with respect to the child under this Act
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR person with parental responsibility
TO surrender or transfer any part of that responsibility to another
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR person who does not have parental responsibility but has care of the child
TO do what is reasonable for the purpose of safeguarding or promoting the child’s welfare
SUBJECT TO the provisions of this Act [source: section-3-5]
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR father
TO acquire parental responsibility for the child
IF he becomes registered as the child’s father, makes a parental responsibility agreement, or the court orders it [source: section-4-1]
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR person who has acquired parental responsibility
TO cease to have that responsibility
UNLESS the court so orders [source: section-4-2A]
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR court
TO appoint an individual to be the child’s guardian
IF the child has no parent with parental responsibility, or a parent, guardian, or special guardian named in a child arrangements order has died [source: section-5-1]
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR parent with parental responsibility
TO appoint another individual to be the child’s guardian in the event of his death
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR person appointed as a child’s guardian
TO have parental responsibility for the child
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR person appointed as a guardian
TO disclaim his appointment
IF it is done by an instrument in writing signed by him and made within a reasonable time of his first knowing that the appointment has taken effect [source: section-6-5]
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR authority or officer of the Service or a Welsh family proceedings officer
TO comply with any request for a report under this section
